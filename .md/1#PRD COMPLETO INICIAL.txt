OPERATIONAL CHALLENGES IDENTIFIED

During the site visit, several construction issues were observed:

Lack of construction safety measures

Workers without proper PPE

No building safety netting

Unclear routing of plumbing through slabs

No technical rooftop area defined for exhaust systems

No clear fire suppression infrastructure identified

Water system uses pumped distribution instead of rooftop tanks

HVAC duct routing not clearly defined

These issues must be addressed by engineering.

VERTICAL LOGISTICS

The rooftop restaurant depends on vertical supply logistics.

A service elevator with capacity 1050 kg exists.

This elevator will transport:

• food supplies
• beverages
• utensils
• waste
• equipment

It must operate separately from guest elevators.
